About Labwise

A single, local supplier for laboratory & science procurement.

Labwise Trading cc is a Namibian-registered close corporation supplying laboratory equipment, glassware, chemicals, consumables and STEAM materials to schools, universities, clinics, research institutions and government departments.

We source from established South African and international manufacturers — which lets us hold competitive prices without compromising specification compliance. Unlike equipment-only competitors, we supply both apparatus and chemicals, simplifying your procurement to one purchase order, one delivery, one invoice.
