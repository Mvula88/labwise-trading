01
Who we are

A Namibian close corporation, built for institutional procurement.

Labwise Trading cc was founded to fix a recurring problem we saw across Namibian education and research: institutions were juggling three or four suppliers to outfit a single laboratory — glassware from one, chemicals from another, PPE from a third, equipment from somewhere overseas.

We consolidated all of it under one roof. Apparatus and reagents on the same quote. One purchase order, one delivery, one invoice. Sourced from established South African and international manufacturers so the specifications hold up against any tender requirement.

Today we supply primary and secondary schools, universities, vocational training colleges, clinics, hospitals, research institutions and government departments across all 14 regions of Namibia.

02
Our mission

Equip every Namibian school and lab with the tools to do real science.

Quality laboratory and science teaching materials, delivered reliably, priced fairly, and backed by people who know education.

That sentence is short on purpose. It's the test we apply to every quote, every delivery, every customer interaction. If we can't honour all four parts — quality, reliability, fair pricing, and education-literate service — we'd rather decline the order than fail the customer.
