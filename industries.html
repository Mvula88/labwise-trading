Who we serve

Built for the institutions that build Namibia.

Education, healthcare, research, vocational training and government — we supply the institutions whose work depends on having the right equipment in the right place at the right time. Below is what we typically supply each sector, and the way we work with them.

  1. 01

    Schools — primary, secondary & combined

    From Grade R science corners to Grade 12 NSSCAS chemistry, biology and physics practical laboratories. We supply against ministerial syllabus requirements and individual school needs — equipping new labs from scratch and topping up existing ones term by term.

    Typically supplied
    Light microscopes Beakers & flasks Bunsen burners & gas cartridges Test tubes & racks Litmus & pH paper Dissection kits Safety goggles & lab coats Standard reagents
  2. 02

    Universities & colleges

    UNAM, NUST, IUM and other tertiary teaching and research labs. Faculty and departmental procurement for undergraduate practicals, postgraduate research, and shared instrumentation. We handle both the volume side (100 student practical kits) and the specification-heavy side (analytical balance with calibration certificate, spectrophotometer with warranty).

    Typically supplied
    Binocular & trinocular microscopes Analytical balances Spectrophotometers Centrifuges Standardised reagents Volumetric glassware (Class A) Bulk consumables Research-grade chemicals
  3. 03

    Vocational training centres & TVET

    Engineering, electrical, electronics, plumbing and trades workshops at VTCs and technical colleges. Includes the deeper electronics and test-equipment items — oscilloscopes, signal generators, soldering stations, antenna and fibre-optic trainers — for advanced TVET programmes.

    Typically supplied
    Soldering & rework stations Digital oscilloscopes Bench multimeters Signal generators DC power supplies Antenna training kits Fibre-optic trainers PCB prototyping machines
  4. 04

    Clinics & hospitals

    Primary care clinics, district hospitals and diagnostic labs — clinical consumables, basic diagnostic equipment, examination supplies and PPE. We supply what the procurement officer asked for, in the quantities they asked for, on the timeline they need.

    Typically supplied
    Nitrile examination gloves Cotton wool & swabs Specimen containers Clinical thermometers Lab coats & PPE First aid kits Disposable pipettes Sterile consumables
  5. 05

    Research institutions

    Government research bodies, agricultural research, environmental testing and specialised monitoring. We source instrumentation outside the standard catalogue too — for example, the Bertin AlphaE radon monitor for occupational radon monitoring (see the catalogue under Specialised Instruments).

    Typically supplied
    Radon monitors Calibration weights Moisture analyzers Specialised glassware Research-grade reagents Sampling equipment Data loggers Custom sourcing on request
  6. 06

    Government departments

    Ministries, regional offices and parastatals procuring science, education and safety supplies — including ministry-of-education science kit rollouts to under-equipped schools. We respond to formal tenders, framework agreements, and direct purchase orders.

    Typically supplied
    Multi-school equipment rollouts Standardised kit lists PPE bulk orders Tender-grade specifications First aid (Reg 7) Regional delivery coordination Vendor documentation Itemised invoicing
